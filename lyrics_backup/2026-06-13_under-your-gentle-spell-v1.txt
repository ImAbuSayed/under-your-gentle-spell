[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Underneath the moonlight, I’m pulling you near
Watching all the shadows slowly disappear
Wrapped up in a moment that I never want to break
Every little move is a chance I want to take

[Verse 1]
Shadows on the floorboards and a crackling fire
Tangled in the silence, giving in to desire
Your eyes hold a secret that I’m dying to read
Showing me a feeling that I didn't know I'd need

[Pre-Chorus]
The hour’s getting late, but we’re taking our time
Pouring out our souls like a glass of sweet wine
A burning sort of magic in the palm of your hand
Taking me away to an undiscovered land

[Chorus]
Oh, I’m falling under your gentle spell
Deeper than the water in a midnight well
Lost inside the gravity of your embrace
Finding my heaven in a quiet place

[Post-Chorus]
Hold me closer, let the night stand still
